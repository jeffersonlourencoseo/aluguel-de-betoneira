2. Audit Categories

2.1 Technical SEO

Score: 87/100

What Works

  • HTTPS/HSTS active with valid Let's Encrypt certificate
  • Canonical non-www to www redirect (308) configured
  • robots.txt allows all crawlers and references sitemap
  • XML sitemap contains 17 valid, indexable URLs
  • Canonical tags and meta robots are consistent across pages
  • Schema JSON-LD present and syntactically valid

Findings

Trailing-slash URLs return 200 instead of 301 High

URL /servicos/betoneira-120l (without slash) returns 200, creating a duplicate indexable version.

Recommendation: Configure server to 301-redirect all slashless URLs to the trailing-slash canonical version.

Breadcrumb schema uses inconsistent URLs Medium

BreadcrumbList item 'Servicos' points to /servicos (no slash) while the real page is /servicos/.

Recommendation: Update all breadcrumb item URLs to match canonical trailing-slash URLs.

Missing security headers Low

Content-Security-Policy and X-Frame-Options are not present.

Recommendation: Add CSP and X-Frame-Options headers for additional security.

2.2 Content Quality & E-E-A-T

Score: 58/100

What Works

  • Service pages include technical specification tables
  • Calculator tool provides utility value to users
  • Privacy policy and terms pages exist
  • Blog covers commercial intent topics (price, location, monthly rental)

Findings

Inconsistent technical specifications for 400L model Critical

Service page lists 3 CV / 480 kg while blog posts list 2 CV / 520 kg for the same 400L mixer.

Recommendation: Create a single source of truth for specs and update all pages accordingly.

About page uses generic unverifiable team names High

Names like 'Joao Silva', 'Ana Paula Costa', 'Marcos Rocha' appear without photos, LinkedIn, or professional registrations.

Recommendation: Replace with real team profiles including photos, roles, tenure, and CREA/registrations where applicable.

Blog posts lack real author bylines High

Author is generic brand name or absent; reduces authority signals.

Recommendation: Assign real authors and add Person schema.

Testimonials lack verification attributes High

No photos, cities, dates, or links to verify the three homepage testimonials.

Recommendation: Use real customer reviews from Google Business or Reclame Aqui and mark them up with Review schema.

Year inconsistency in pricing article Medium

Title/H1 mentions '2026' but an H2 inside the article says '2024'.

Recommendation: Review and update all dates to the current year.

2.5 Core Web Vitals & Performance

Score: 74/100

What Works

  • TTFB ~60 ms (excellent)
  • CLS near 0 (excellent)
  • TBT 0 ms and low main-thread interference
  • Brotli compression and long-term cache headers active

Findings

Render-blocking resources delay LCP High

Google Fonts CSS and Astro CSS block rendering, causing ~1.4-1.6 s element render delay.

Recommendation: Load Google Fonts asynchronously; inline critical CSS and defer non-critical styles.

Hero image is intentionally blurred and low quality High

LCP element is a blurred JPEG placeholder, hurting perceived quality.

Recommendation: Replace with a sharp, optimized WebP/AVIF photo of a real mixer on a job site.

Images served as JPEG instead of modern formats Medium

Hero and team photos are JPEG; WebP/AVIF would reduce size.

Recommendation: Use Astro Picture component with WebP/AVIF fallback.

2.9 Visual / Mobile UX

Score: 64/100

What Works

  • Site is responsive and uses large typography
  • Contact form is mobile-friendly
  • No intrusive interstitials or popups

Findings

Hero H1 too large on mobile High

text-6xl H1 pushes trust badges and secondary CTA below the fold.

Recommendation: Reduce H1 to text-4xl/text-5xl on mobile and ensure CTA/badge visibility above the fold.

Primary and WhatsApp colors fail WCAG contrast High

#E8A838 on white = 2.08:1; white on #25D366 = 1.98:1.

Recommendation: Darken primary/CTA colors or increase font weight/size to meet WCAG AA.

Tap targets below 44 px Medium

Hamburger, footer links, and social icons are smaller than recommended.

Recommendation: Increase padding and min-height to at least 44 px for all interactive elements.
